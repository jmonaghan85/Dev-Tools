🔧 Toggle Bookmarklet

Override feature toggle values on BBC web pages via ?toggles=name:true,name:false.

Installation

Add your toggle names below, then drag the button into your bookmarks bar. If your bookmarks bar isn't visible press Cmd+Shift+B (Mac) / Ctrl+Shift+B (Windows).
