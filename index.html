🧪 Experiment Bookmarklet

A browser bookmarklet for overriding ?experiments=name:variant on BBC web pages.

Installation

Drag the button below into your browser's bookmarks bar. If your bookmarks bar isn't visible, press Cmd+Shift+B (Mac) or Ctrl+Shift+B (Windows) to show it.

⚗️ BBC Experiments

Drag this to your bookmarks bar ↑  |  Click it on any BBC web page to open the experiment picker.

Features: pre-loaded experiments & variants · custom experiment entry · active experiment highlighted · localStorage saves your custom entries · clear button to remove the override.
